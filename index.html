Robots and Abilities

Zenith

Abilities:

  • Ability: Astral Shield - Zenith conjures a protective astral shield around itself and nearby allies.
  • Description: Upon activation, Zenith creates a shimmering astral shield that surrounds itself and allies within a designated radius. The astral shield absorbs incoming damage, providing increased resistance to enemy attacks for Zenith and its allies.
  • Cooldown: 16 sec
  • Duration: 8 sec
  • Shield Radius: 35 meters

Statistics:

  • Health: 102,350
  • Speed: 42 KPH

Nexus

    Chrono Field

    Upon activation, Nexus emits a powerful field that absorbs a percentage of the incoming damage. Allies in this field will also receive the protection. After the Chrono Field ends, all allies including Nexus will receive GREY HP repair if they were in the range of the field.

    Cooldown: 16 sec

    Duration: 8 sec

    Field Radius: 100 meters

    Damage Reduced: 45%

Statistics:

    Health: 113,000

    Speed: 39 KPH

Luchador

Defense Points: 35% (70 defense points)

Luchador is a very tanky Titan. Having a great amount of defense points, this Titan's abilities are meant to keep him alive.

    Frog Leap

    Titan jumps traveling a long distance thanks to its long afterburners being able to push that much weight. During the jump, Luchador is protected by a reflector. During landing, Luchador is able to send an explosive shockwave that deals damage and applies the Supression effect to enemies that were struck.

    Cooldown: 15 sec.

    Supression: 5 sec, (50%)

    Base Damage: 37,550

    Ultimate Defense

    Luchador becomes immobilized after a very short delay, however, Luchador activates a powerful Reflector that blocks incoming damage and reflects it back to the enemies that damage Luchador. After the end of the ability, Luchador admisses 3 healing pulses that heal allies in range and Luchador himself.

    Cooldown: 14 sec.

    Duration: 6 sec

    Reflector: Base Damage Block: 65%, Damage Reflected: 50%

    Healing: 15,000 per pulse.

Statistics:

    Health: 542,500

    Speed: 30 KPH

Paragon

    Stampede

    When ability is activcated, Paragon starts to charge in the direction the robot is facing. During the charge, Paragon creates a magnetic field that damages and pushes enemies away from him. Paragon also turns some incoming damage into DOT, allowing him to heal most of the damage dealt during the durability. After the charge ends, Paragon remains creating an magnetic field, but doesn't move as fast.

    Cooldown: 14 sec

    Charge Duration: 8 sec.

    Afterwave Duration: 6 sec.

    Convertion: 80% damage = DOT

Statistics:

    Health: 142,550

    Speed: 39 KPH
